Are you ready for the Revival?

Revival Brewcade is unique new brewery, unlike any other in Canada. We are located in the heart of barley country, Calgary, Alberta. Our goal is Revive the kid in you with our retro 80s Arcade games and Pinball, while retaining the adult in you with small-batch Craft Beer.

We pride ourselves at being the first and only Craft Brewery Arcade in Canada!

Owners James & Jamie are long time friends who are passionate about Arcades and Beer. We grew up during the 80s and 90s Arcade era. James is a successful entrepreneur with several boutique candy stores. Jamie is an award winning home brewer specializing in IPAs.

The Arcade

We focus on classic Arcade games and Pinball with a modern twist. Come play Pacmac, Centipede, X-Men, Street Fighter, Ninja Turtles and more. For the Pinheads we have Ghost busters, Walking dead, south park and more.

Play with your drinking buddies or co-workers on the worlds only 10-player arcade strategy game, Killer Queen.

The Beer

Our goal is to bring small batch brews to Calgary, focusing on massively late hopped juicy IPAs, traditional American and English style ales, experimental and one-offs. To give back to the home brewing community, we will have a dedicated tap with the Alberta home brewers award winning beer.

Not a beer fan? No problem! We offer wine, spirits and cider. If you are alcohol free we have a wide range of sodas and water

So come have a pint, play some games and enjoy!

Revival Brewcade Beers

Fruit Juice Smoothie DIPA

A massively late hopped double IPA that will punish your tongue at 8.5% ABV. At 140 calculated IBU this Citra / Amarillo monster is ready to scare you like Fred Savage in the movie Little Monsters.

Paper Boy IPA

Western-style IPA with Alberta 2-row focusing on citrus, floral and dank. Made with Centennial, Cascade and Columbus hops.

Flight of the Galaxy IPA

7% 85IBU - Falconers Flight and Galaxy hops are the focus of this IPA. Big citrus, juice, orange, with tropical aromas.

Sunny Side Up Pale Ale

A new england style pale ale with lots of citrus, fruit flavours, without the hop bite.

Crushable Wheat

Easy drinking wheat beer.

Continental Divide Dry Stout

4% - An irish-style dry stout made with Red Shed Alberta barley and UK pale.

Citra Blonde Ale

A flavorful blonde ale made with the wonderful Citra hop. Easy drinking and tropical flavours.

Seasonals and Experimentals

We love experimenting with beer, and our small nano system allows us to do things that other craft breweries would consider risky. Our goal is to feature experimental and seasonal rotation beers weekly.

Gluten Reduced

We will offer gluten reduced beer as seasonal options for our celiac friends. While this isn’t truly gluten free.
